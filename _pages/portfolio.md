## Featured Projects

### Slump Dog Sluggers - Philadelphia Phillies Performance Analysis

**Challenge:** Analyze whether age-related decline impacts player performance across the 2024-2025 seasons.

**Approach:** Built a Power BI dashboard tracking multi-timeframe rolling averages (7-game, 14-game, 30-game) to identify performance trends and test age-decline hypotheses.

**Tools:** Power BI, SQL Server, Excel

**Key Insight:** Data challenged initial assumptions about age-related decline, revealing more nuanced patterns in player performance over time.

### Fantasy Football AI Decision-Making Experiment

**Challenge:** Compare AI-assisted decision-making systems for fantasy football lineup optimization over a 17-week season.

**Approach:** Conducted controlled experiment comparing ChatGPT vs Claude recommendations, tracking variance, win rates, and decision confidence across multiple decision points per week.

**Tools:** Python, Excel, Data Visualization

**Key Insight:** Demonstrated structured approach to experimental design and variance tracking in decision-support systems.
